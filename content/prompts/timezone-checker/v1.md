- 24-hour grid with day/night indicator (night: 00–06, 20–23 dimmed; day: 07–19 bright)
- Green highlight on hours overlapping primary zone's 09–18 working hours

- Click card header → set as primary (distinct visual treatment)

**Settings** (⚙ icon, localStorage): 12h / 24h time format, configurable working hours and wake-up window.
